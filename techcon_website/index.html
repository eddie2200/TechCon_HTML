What is TechCon 2024 conference?

TechCon Kenya 2024 is a national technology and innovation conference that brings together Kenya’s brightest minds in tech, business, and academia.
Scheduled to take place at the Kenyatta International Convention Centre (KICC), Nairobi.
This 3-day event will focus on advancing homegrown solutions, showcasing Kenyan innovation, and connecting stakeholders across the digital economy.

Theme: "Tech for Impact – Innovation Beyond Borders"

The 2024 theme reflects Kenya’s growing role as a tech hub in Africa — from the heart of Nairobi’s Silicon Savannah to emerging innovation hubs in Kisumu, Eldoret, and Mombasa.
TechCon Kenya provides a platform for entrepreneurs, developers, policy makers, investors and students to explore how digital tools can solve real-world challenges across sectors like agriculture,
health, finance, education, and transport.
